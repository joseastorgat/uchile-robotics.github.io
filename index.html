What We Do

UChile Robotics is currently formed by 3 teams, UChile Homebreakers, Pepper Team and UChile Robotics SPL Team. All teams work at the Robotics Laboratory of the Electrical Department of the University of Chile.

Our Robots

Bender

Bender is a humanoid robot designed for human-robot interaction at home-like environments. He uses his computer vision, manipulation and navigation capabilities to help at home and solve a wide set of tasks.

It is developed by the UChile Homebreakers Team since 2007. He usually participates in the international robotics competition Robocup. He has reach the 5th place among 24 teams.

Nao Team

The SPL team has been the fourth best team in the league by three years in a row. It is formed by about 15 people pursuing bachelor, masters and doctorate programs in electrical engineering and computer science.

Research developed by the team is related to perception, localization, decision making and multi-robot systems with resources-constrained platforms in highly dynamic environments.

Pepper

Pepper is the last robot to join our family. The team focuses on human-robot interaction for home environments.

The team is currently working hard on him for the upcoming Robocup competition.
